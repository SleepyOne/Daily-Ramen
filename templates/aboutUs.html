About Us

We created this site in our CPSC 350 class in college. As you may know, college is a time where
you often feel like you're starving to death due to the lack of quality food. Because of this
we decided it was time to create a site that would help solve this problem. Our site contains
thousands of recipes with short ingredient lists, intended to help the typical broke college
student survive the daily grind. We hope you enjoy it!
